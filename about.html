About Me

Hey, I’m an aspiring programmer — a curious mind drawn to the quiet magic of code. I love how simple logic can bring complex ideas to life, how structure becomes expression. I’m still learning, but I learn fast, and the process itself keeps me inspired.

Right now, I’m deepening my skills one project at a time, exploring the languages and patterns that shape the digital world. It feels like learning to speak in a new form — and I’ve always had a love for languages.

Beyond Code

When I’m not coding, I write — poetry, short stories, and fragments of thought. Words help me understand the inner world the same way code helps shape the outer one.

I’ve also trained in Neuro-Linguistic Programming, resilience coaching, and systemic constellation work — ways of exploring how we think, feel, and make meaning. In a way, it’s just another kind of programming — this time, of the mind.

Code mirrors my curiosity for how things work; language holds meaning — both are part of how I move through the world.
